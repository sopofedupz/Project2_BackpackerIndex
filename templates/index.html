The team has worked to pull together some information of the top 137 global destinations that backpackers and travel lovers can travel to after covid-19 is over. The information were obtained and scraped from PriceofTravel.

Per PriceofTravel, the cities are ranked using a daily total value calculation, which includes:

  • a dorm bed at a good and cheap hostel
  • 3 budget meals
  • 2 public transportation rides
  • 1 paid cultural attraction (please refer to PriceofTravel for more information)
  • 3 cheap beers (as an "entertainment fund")

Source: PriceofTravel
